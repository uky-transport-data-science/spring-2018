The project:

Pattern of Delays in Commuter Rail Data

The Metra is a commuter rail that shuttles people from suburbs

in Michigan, Indiana, Wisconsin and Illinois to Chicago.

Metra publishes an API, which they are beta testing, that

tracks schedule information-including current train delays.

When compiled this information could be mapped to depict the

frequency with which delays occur by station.
