My Research

There are approximately 36,000 traffic related deaths annually. 94% of these deaths are due to human error. There are multiple ways to resolve this problem, but one way is to use connected and autonomous vehicles. I am currently researching the safety implications of the transition to connected and autonomous vehicles. To do this, I must show how connected and autonomous features can help save lives. I have the Kentucky crash database, which I can merge with the various levels of autonomy and show how certain features can prevent certain crashes, ultimately bringing automobile related deaths to zero.
